What is your rate?

Every photo is as unique as clients requirements. In this case careful estimation is the only solution to achieve win-win situation both for the client and service provider. Please send me the images you have together with the detailed instruction and I will figure out the best offer I can make for you. There are discounts for long-term partnership and big batches of images.

How can we pay you?

I accept paypal, bank cards and wire transfers.

Where are you based?

The company is based in Vilnius, Lithuania, northern Europe.

I have an urgent project. How fast do you work?

I work pretty fast but due to the current work load I am not able to perform urgent projects. Please contact me at least two days ahead of the deadline.

How can I send you the files?

We can use file sharing services like Dropbox or FTP.

What file format should I send you?

I work with every kind of graphic files (JPG, TIFF etc) but prefer RAW files in case of precise color correction.

My main tool is Adobe Photoshop CC as part of Creative Cloud. Capture One RAW converter, Topaz Labs plug-ins are also in use.
